Online Training

We are a friendly online companion and service provider offering services anytime and anywhere as per your ease around the globe. We have a dedicated team of experts for your guidance and support throughout the session of the course. The course pattern is built for learner of any knowledge-level with clear message that can be easily understood. Every day our instructor will connect with you through a live session at your customized scheduled time for an hour and will train you in that course. Each of the courses have different charges and depends on the category you are opting for.

Basic courses 30days to 45 days.

Advanced courses 60days to 90days.

Job-oriented courses 15days to 20 days.

Specialized Courses 6 months to 9months.

Also we provide options for Personal training for Individuals and Batch Trainings for Individuals, Corporate professionals and other Employees as per your convenience.


Personal Training

The trainer will train you, discuss with you and take your queries into account and will help you in reaching your goals. You will have an exclusive real time feedback opportunity for your performance from your instructor.

Batch Training

The trainer will train a group of people in a live session. The size generally ranges from 4-8 members at a time who gets trained together in an interactive session. The members will get the training as well as can have discussion and clear their doubts effectively. In this type of training, we make sure to start the session once we reach our appropriate batch size.

Once the course comes to an end, you will get an access to our LMS where you will receive all the recorded lectures and a chance to further clarify your doubts. You will also be rewarded with a Certificate stating the successful completion of the course having the name of your trainer which can be utilized to validate your candidature for upcoming job opportunities.
