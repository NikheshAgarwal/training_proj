Refund Policy

Read the following details for the Online Course Service carefully before asking your refund order that cannot be given (see section C below).

Request for refund within 3 days of purchasing the course.

  • For Instructor Led Online Training:

If you want to drop your order and require a refund then you have to raise a refund request within 3 days of purchase of the online course. Money back guarantee is mostly avoided if the participant has attended the online session for 2 days or more. After 3 days of duration, no refund will be provided.

  • Cancellation of events

A K S Training Cum Placement Pvt. Ltd. owns all the right to postpone/terminate an event, or change the location of an event because of lack of enrollments, instructor’s bad health or natural events (like floods, earthquakes, political instability and so on.)

In case A K S Training Cum Placement Pvt. Ltd drops an event, 100% refund will be provided.

If a cancellation is done by a delegate:

a) If a cancellation is done by a delegate within 3 days (or less) before start of the event, full amount will be refunded.

b) If a cancellation is done by a delegate after 3 days of the event, no refunds will be made.

Once the training starts by the instructor, no further refunds will be made.

  • How to raise a Refund request?

Please contact our support team through mail at support@akstrainingcumplacement.com.

  • Refunds: Duplicate payment

Refund of the duplicate payment made by the delegate will be processed via the same source (original method of payment) in 10 upcoming working days soon after intimation by the customer.

Note:

All the refunds will process via the same source (original method of payment) in 10 working days after the refund request is accepted by A K S Training Cum Placement Pvt. Ltd. support team.

If you come across any further issues please contact support@akstrainingcumplacement.com.
