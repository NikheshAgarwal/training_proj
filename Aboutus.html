ABOUT US

Get started with your online learning as well as training experience with us!

We are a training & placement service provider available at your service 24/7 throughout the globe. Our priorities reside in providing you the best possible training experience. All you need to do is enroll in the course of your choice and upgrade your skill-sets. Build your professional career with us. We offer a variety of training programs to College students, Graduates, Professionals and Employees. More than 60+ different Carrier-Building Paid courses are available for you.


The mode of training is both Offline and Online. Live training sessions are conducted at your scheduled time, to have a real-time interactions with the trainer. We offer Learning Management system to you once you complete your course. To help you towards your growth and success, our team is continuously available 24/7. Online mode is available in the form of both Batch and Personal training to achieve your career goals at a faster pace. Offline training is conducted in our Institute premises only, led by a team of Experts for your training in various batches depending on the course.


Some of the specialized training services that we offer are:

  • Corporate Training for employees to develop new skills adopted by the businesses.
  • Personality development training for College students, Fresher and Employees who desire to stand out and work on their communication skills as well as boost up their inter-personal skills.
  • Paid internships in different courses for College students and Fresher to build practical skills and crafting your knowledge into practicality.
  • We have tie-ups with different reputed Organizations and offer paid services for placements also. For this purpose we have a team of experts in our Placement cell who can help you in getting suitable job opportunities.

By the end of each and every training program, you will be provided with a Certificate having the name of your trainer, which you can showcase for getting a job. Connect with us at support@akstrainingcumplacement.com for queries and more information
