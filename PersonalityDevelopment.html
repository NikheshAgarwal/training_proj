Personality Development

We believe that the soft skills are equally important as the Technical skills, before stepping into any Industry or whether you are a person starting your business. Soft skills usually refers to overall personality development. Be it working on your communication skills, social intelligence or your career attributes.

This course aims to make you lead towards the success and being your companion on your success story. We help you to increase your impact on people, workplace and the business. It will guide you to improve your communication and the inter-personal skills required for managerial positions. We help you reach your full potential and ways to boost your self-confidence which is essential in roles of marketing or in any sort of business negotiations.

We understand in today’s competitive world, everyone wants to have a dynamic as well as interactive personality that cast a positive influence on others. It is important to send out a rightful and clear message with purpose whether you are a Fresher or an Owner of a company. From training on how to be productive, organized, building your inter-personal skills in achieving your goal-oriented skills, we got you covered.

We provide training in both online and classroom modes. The learners ranges from College students, Fresher, Employees or a Startup owner. We have batches for different category of people. Our course takes around a week to complete and successfully train you.

For this course, we charge INR 20,000/-per person. And if you are a company looking for training for your employees then the charges ranges up to INR 75,000/- per batch.

Once the course session ends, you will be awarded with a course completion certificate validating the skills you have mastered during the course.
